Welcome to our Resources page! Here you will find a collection of useful links and information to help you on your web development journey. Before joining us at Hot Beans, you are required to complete the following training modules:

  • HTML Fundamentals
  • CSS Styling
  • JavaScript Basics
  • Responsive Design

Here are the roles you can apply for and what you will do in these roles:

  • Web Developer

    • As a Web Developer, you will be responsible for building and maintaining our website. You will work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a seamless user experience.

  • UI/UX Designer

    • As a UI/UX Designer, you will be responsible for creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. You will work with design tools to prototype and iterate on design concepts.

  • Content Creator

    • As a Content Creator, you will be responsible for developing engaging and informative content for our website and marketing materials. You will work with copywriters and designers to create compelling narratives.

    A base qualification for all roles is that you have all of your GCSEs at grade 4 or above. and a base understanding of the subject matter.
